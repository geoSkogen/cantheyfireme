At-will(pg. 16)

We are an "at-will" employer. That means you are free to quit at any time, for any reason, just as New Seasons Market is free to terminate your employment at any time, for any reason not prohibited by law. No staff member or company representative, other than the president/CEO or a person designated by him or her in a signed employment agreement, has the authority to change the at-will employment relationship or to contract with any staff member for different terms of employment.
